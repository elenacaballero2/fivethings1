Five Things About Being a Twin

"Two unique souls united by birth."

"Being a twin is like never having to be alone, you have a best friend created for you."

We get asked some really weird but funny questions, for example:

  • Can you feel eachother's pain, like if I punch you can the other one feel it?
  • Can you read eachother's minds?
  • Who's the evil twin?
  • Are you the same person?
  • Can I call you thing 1 and thing 2?

She is the opposite of me, we compliment each other.

"Yes, it does bother us when you call us thing 1 and thing 2."

Having a twin sister is a blessing. I always have someone to talk to and hang out with. We grew up loving the same things like singing, acting, and being creative. Our dream is to travel the world and just be creative together.
